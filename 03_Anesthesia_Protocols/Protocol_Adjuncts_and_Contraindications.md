# Adjuncts, Contraindications, and Safety Notes

## Opioid-Free Adjuncts
- Ketamine: avoid in uncontrolled HTN, psychosis
- Dexmedetomidine: caution in HR <50, conduction blocks
- Magnesium: avoid in myasthenia gravis, renal failure

## Regional Anesthesia Contraindications
- ACB/IPACK: ASRA timing rules (table included)
- Local infection, patient refusal, coagulopathy

## Opioid-Based Contraindications
- Chronic respiratory disease
- Known OSA requiring CPAP

## Agents to Avoid
- Gabapentin/pregabalin (ASA/APSF 2024)
