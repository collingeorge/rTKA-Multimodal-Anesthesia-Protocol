# Quick Reference – OFIA With ACB+IPACK
• Pre-op: Tylenol 1g, Celecoxib 400 mg, Dex 10 mg, Scopolamine
• Blocks: ACB 20–30 mL 0.2–0.25%; IPACK 20 mL 0.2–0.25%
• Induction: Prop 2 mg/kg, Ket 0.3 mg/kg, Roc 0.6 mg/kg
• Maintenance: Dex 0.4 mcg/kg/h, Ket 0.1 mg/kg/h, Mg 40 mg/kg
• Emergence: Stop Dex 10–15 min, Sugammadex 2 mg/kg
• PACU: Tylenol/NSAIDs; Ketamine rescue 0.1 mg/kg
